What Is It?

How a Dental Implant Works

1
🔩

Titanium Post

A biocompatible titanium screw is placed into the jawbone — acting as the artificial tooth root that fuses with bone over time.

2
⚙️

Abutment

A connector piece — the abutment — is placed on top of the titanium post once it has integrated with your jawbone.

3
😁

Ceramic Crown

A custom-made, natural-looking ceramic crown is fitted on top — indistinguishable from your real teeth in look and function.

Our Process

Your Implant Journey at Xpress Dent

1

Free Consultation & 3D Scan

Our implant specialist evaluates your jawbone density, gum health, and overall oral condition using a 3D CBCT scan for precise treatment planning.

2

Implant Placement

Under local anaesthesia, the titanium implant is precisely placed into the jawbone. The procedure is completely painless and takes about 30–60 minutes.

3

Osseointegration

Over 3–6 months, the titanium post fuses with your jawbone (osseointegration) — creating a strong, permanent foundation.

4

Crown Placement & Final Smile

Your custom ceramic crown is placed — perfectly matched to your natural teeth. Your new smile is ready to wow the world!

Implant FAQs

Am I suitable for dental implants? +
Most adults with good general health and adequate jawbone are suitable. Our specialist will assess during your free consultation using a 3D scan.
Is the implant procedure painful? +
No. The procedure is done under local anaesthesia and you will not feel any pain during the surgery. Mild discomfort for a day or two post-procedure is normal and easily managed with medication.
How long do dental implants last? +
With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. The crown on top may need replacement after 15–20 years, but the implant itself is designed to be permanent.
What is the cost of a dental implant at Xpress Dent? +
Cost depends on your individual case, bone density, and implant type. Book a free consultation and we'll give you a transparent, detailed quote with no hidden charges.
