What Is It?

The Art & Science of Smile Makeover

Smile Design is a multi-step cosmetic dental process that analyses your facial structure, tooth shape, lip line, and gum appearance to create a personalised blueprint for your perfect smile.

Using cutting-edge Digital Smile Design (DSD) software, we show you a realistic preview of your new smile before a single tooth is touched. Our specialists then use a combination of veneers, bonding, whitening, contouring, and gum reshaping to bring that preview to life.

Treatments

Everything in Your Smile Makeover

🪞

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin shells of porcelain bonded to the front of teeth — correcting colour, shape, size and alignment in one go. Stain-resistant and lifelike.

🎨

Composite Bonding

Tooth-coloured resin applied and sculpted directly on teeth — a quick, affordable way to fix chips, gaps, and discolouration.

✨

Teeth Whitening

Professional laser whitening that removes deep stains and brightens your smile by several shades in a single 60-minute session.

💎

Ceramic Crowns

Full coverage caps for damaged or severely discoloured teeth — custom milled in our clinic for a perfect, same-day fit.

📐

Gum Contouring

Laser reshaping of uneven or excessive gum tissue to reveal more tooth surface and frame your smile perfectly.

🦷

Orthodontic Alignment

Clear aligners or traditional braces to straighten teeth as part of a comprehensive smile makeover plan.
